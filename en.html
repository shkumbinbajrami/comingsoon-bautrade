What is Bautrade?

Bautrade is a digital auction platform for the construction industry. Companies, traders and private individuals buy and sell building materials, machinery, tools and other construction products – simply, transparently and efficiently.

Who is Bautrade for?

For companies in construction

Bautrade is the auction platform for the construction industry. Buy and sell building materials, machinery, tools and other construction products – easily, securely and digitally.

For trading companies in the sector

Present your range on Bautrade and reach companies and professionals in the construction industry. Sell materials, machinery and construction accessories via a modern auction platform.

For DIY enthusiasts

Whether renovation, conversion or personal project – on Bautrade you'll find building materials, machinery, tools and everything you need for your project. Benefit from attractive offers and a simple purchasing process.
